Welcome to Market Communications of Minnesota!

Our mission is to support individuals in achieving their employment goals through comprehensive, personalized services. We specialize in helping clients, including those referred by Vocational Rehabilitation Services, to navigate their path to meaningful employment or entrepreneurship.

Resume Building and Job Search Assistance

We create tailored resumes that highlight your skills and experiences. Our guidance on job search strategies and interview preparation will help you stand out to employers.

Career Planning and Coaching

Our career coaches offer one-on-one support to help you identify your strengths, set career goals, and develop a clear plan to achieve them.

Starting a New Business

We provide resources and expertise to help you turn your business idea into reality, from developing a business plan to managing small business certifications.

Vocational Rehabilitation Support

We partner with Vocational Rehabilitation Services to assist clients with disabilities or other barriers to employment. Our customized plans ensure you receive the support you need to succeed.
