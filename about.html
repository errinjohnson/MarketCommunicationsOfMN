About Market Communications of Minnesota

At Market Communications of Minnesota, we are dedicated to empowering individuals and businesses through effective communication, business development, and career support services. Our mission is to help clients achieve their employment goals, whether they are looking to improve their resume, explore new career paths, or start a business.

Our Services

  • Career Support: Personalized career coaching, resume building, and job search assistance.
  • Business Development: Support for entrepreneurs, including business planning and small business certification.
  • Vocational Rehabilitation Support: Assistance for clients facing employment barriers, in partnership with Vocational Rehabilitation Services.
